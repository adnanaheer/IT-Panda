ERP

ERP is the process used by companies with another to form a whole almost all the aspects of and factors of a company from HR to finances. ERP has played a great role on industry level as well as small business. ERP may be a process employed by companies to manage and combine with another to form a whole the important parts of their businesses. Many ERP software applications are very important to companies because they assist them implement resource planning by integrating all of the processes needed to run their companies with one system. An ERP software also can plan, purchasing inventory, sales, marketing, finance, human resources. ERP is business management software-usually a suite of integrated applications that a company can use to store and manage data from every stage of business, including: production planning, cost development, marketing and sales, inventory management, shipping, payment.
